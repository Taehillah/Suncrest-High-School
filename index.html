Mission and Vision Statement
"The mission of this school is to foster academic, social and extra-curricular growth through the development of cognitive thinking skills. We provide educational opportunities for all cultural groups in our community and serve its diverse needs by promoting a culture of thinking and learning. We undertake to: ﻿﻿promote cognitive thinking ﻿﻿teach skills for problem solving ﻿﻿develop intrinsic motivation and reflective thinking ﻿﻿provide structures to develop the total person ﻿﻿nurture a spirit of tolerance and honesty ﻿﻿respond to the needs of the market place and the broader community ﻿﻿uphold a set of values and norms based on respect for the dignity of all life."
Ms Dlamini / Principal, Suncrest High School
